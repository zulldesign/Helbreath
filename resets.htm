Resets



Resets occur twice a week on Monday and Thursday mornings. They take anywhere from 15 to 120 mins, any scheduled maintenance is posted as an announcement on the front page of the website.

Monday Resets: Focuses on delivering online store orders, rejuv tokens and completing tickets.

Friday Resets: Focuses on delivering online store orders maintenance of the server.
