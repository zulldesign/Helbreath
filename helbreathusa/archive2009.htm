Beta Testers!

We have an exciting opportunity ahead of us and we need you to make it happen!
 
We need 25 players to be testers of a new feature for the game. For those interested please contact gamemaster with the following information: Name, login id, email.
 
The testers will be provided with test accounts to create a character and play on. (We reserve the right to adjust as needed to do the testing we require). There will also be a testing forum section on Helbreath Portal to facilitate communication amongst the team.
 
The test accounts and testing forum accounts will be provided by iENT and will be cleared and locked at the end of testing.
 
There is no compensation for this beyond the thanks of a grateful game community. The testing has already begun from the end of the reset on March 5th, 2009 and the testing is expected to be completed on or before the start of the reset on March 26th, 2009.
 
If youre interested in helping us test this huge step please email from your most active email address to gamemaster and include your real name, main account login. If accepted well email you back with the non-disclosure agreement for you to sign in order to get the login information.
 
Applications will close soon
