Weapon Damage

We are currently holding a server wide test with all weapons, increasing their damage by D1, EG: 1D2 becomes 1D3. All weapons have had their small and large dice increased. There were a few other alterations noted below.

  • Arrows had their dice increased by 1D2, raising it from 1D4 to 2D5. (Bows have also been increased by D1)
  • The Devastator has had its dice damage increased overall raising it from 4D8+1 to 4D10+2 (including the D1 upgrade).

This weapon upgrade includes all Swords (short and long), Axes, Hammers (normal, battle and barbarian), bows, arrows and most wands.

There is no time limit on this test and any / all changes could remain permanent.

Update:

The following weapons have yet to be increased in damage, technical difficulties prevented us from being able to upgrade all damage.

Hammer, Battle Hammer, Giant Battle Hammer, Barbarian Hammer, Black Shadow Sword, Storm Bringer, The Devastator, Dark Executor, Lightening Blade, Kloness Blade, Kloness Axe, Kloness Esterk, Fire Bow and Direction Bow.

Game Master Criminal Characters

Some Game Masters have been given an additional character to hold small events on. The purpose of this event is for the players to attack the Game Master character, killing them in order to get a 2/3 Zemstone of Sacrifice drop. During March 2008 the Game Master Criminal characters will appear randomly.

Attacking a Game Master character:
If you wish to engage in a fight with the Game Master and attempt to get their Zemstone, you can hit the Game Master when ever you wish. By hitting the Criminal character you are saying that you are willing to be killed by the Game Master and acknowledge the risks in doing so, which includes being ambushed by players from the other town.

Having a Game Master stop the attack:
Should you want the Game Master to stop attacking, you must say Uncle. Be aware that you might have to repeat this a few times in order for the attack to stop. Dying due to your initial attack on the Game Master even when saying Uncle is at your own risk any loss of Zemstones or items will not be able to be replaced.

Note: Attacking the Game Master and saying Uncle straight away will not always result in the Game Master stopping the attacks. The consequence of attacking and then calling out Uncle straight away to taunt the Game Master will be contribution reduced to 0 (Zero), PK count made to be -10, reputation reduced by 1,000 and your EK count halved for each violation.
