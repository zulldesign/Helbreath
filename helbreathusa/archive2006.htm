Be careful with your items

TIP: When you wish to drop an item there is a screen that confirms your request. This asked for your safety and is working as intended and not a bug.

If you slide an item out of your inventory once, click no to the verification screen and then repeat sliding the item out later while the option is ON your item WILL drop, due to the check being ON and being previously asked.

To change the option you have to slide something out of your inventory that's different to what you've previously done and then turn the safety check option OFF.

For your own safety leave the option to OFF.
