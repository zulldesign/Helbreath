Special Offers!

 

Every now and then we have special offers that appear in the online store and PayPal, see below for more information!

  • +Brittle Items


    The term Brittle is given to items as a method of knowing which items in game are “limited usage“, much like Bishop items are. Meaning that they will be in game for as long as their endurance lasts, then no longer be usable by the character. There is no set schedule for the items to be removed from the game. The items will be bound to the character that they are bought for to avoid players trading inadvertently for an item that has little to no endurance. To check out what item is currently in the online store, click here.

  • +Fading Items


    The term Fading is given to items as a method of knowing which items in game are only there for a limited time, once the time has expired the item will be removed from game (last reset of the month, regardless of when the item was bought) . There will be a schedule for the items to be in game (see the calendar), along with a little bit of notice for the next fading item and their  eventual removal. The items will be bound to the character that they are bought for to avoid players trading inadvertently for an item that expires the next day.To check out what item is currently in the online store, click here.
