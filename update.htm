  • +Reset - 7th Feb - New client and other info!


    The next reset will require you to run the auto updater in order to login to the game. I’m aware that some people have had issues with the auto updater so I’ll be including a patch file which I’ll post later.

    • The alchemy recipes to make spell manuals from parchments are now all like this:
      Parchment I + II + II + Slime Jelly + Mana Potion
    • Control T, enables and disables chat. Meaning that if you’re currently in a whisper with someone and you press control + T with your mouse NOT hovering over a player your whisper will be removed.
    • Control P, sends a request to party and also removes yourself from party. Meaning that if you’re NOT in a party and you press control + P over the character, it will send the request. If you ARE in a party and press control + P it will remove you from the party.
    • A new “shout” mode has been added to the F12 menu. Previously there was On | Of, meaning that you either had all chat or nothing. A new mode has been enabled called Town. The Town chat will be the default setting of ALL clients. This means that shout (red) chat is disabled unless you turn it on (I don’t believe that this feature is able to be saved in the settings, so it will need to be done each time you load the client).
    • A monsters “death blow” has been corrected and now shows a “!” at the end of the damage indicating that they’ve died.
    • Fire Pixies will now look like Fire Pixies, not Dummy’s. Mana has been increased.
    • Earth Pixies will look like Earth Pixies, not Stalkers. Mana has been increased.
    • The hunger messages have been altered in an attempt to stand out more.

  • +Reset - 24th Nov - Happy Thanksgiving!


    During the reset we will be implementing a new client. This means that you will have to run the Helbreath.exe (if you already don’t) in order to be able to connect to the server.

    If the file doesn’t download, any of the files listed, you may have to load your Helbreath file and delete the existing version, then run the auto updater (Helbreath.exe) in order to initiate downloading them.

    Files that have been updated

    • HelGame.exe
      You must have the latest version of the client in order to be able to connect to the server.
    • SPRITES/Item-Pack.Pak
    •  SPRITES/Item-Ground.Pak 
      We’ve also made the leathers a little brighter in order to make them easier to see on the ground
    • CONTENTS/ItemName.cfg
    • CONTENTS/contents1.txt
    • CONTENTS/MAGICCFG.TXT

    The Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Wild, Rejuvenation and Portal Tokens have had their images updated. If you do not see an image for them you will need to locate the PAK files mentioned above and delete them in order to force the auto updater to download them.

    Also included in this client is the ability to press Control F and have your co-ordinates broadcast into town (purple / blue) chat.

    Rank 3 Scrolls are now available to buy in game for 9,950,000 gold. Please note that it will not reflect the correct amount being withdrawn in the test to the lower left however the correct amount is taken from your gold. The Rank 3 Scroll is used to turn the new spells obtained through the recent Halloween event back into their scroll form.

    The Halloween Quests will be altered to reflect a more permanent  quest reward. If you wish to complete them in their current form, you have 1 week to complete your quests.

    Incorrect password? You may need to reset it. We’ve completed our forced password changes on the accounts. Try the password recovery system first since it will be faster, other wise complete the form located here - Make sure you complete ALL of the form, don’t answer with “I don’t know”, say what you think it may be!

    Sharing your account? Did you know… that if you share your account with someone who is banned from game, you risk YOUR account being banned! Limit who you give your password to, or better yet keep it to yourself!

  • +Client update 4.15.02


    Please note some things listed below may already be implemented and running the auto updater will be required in order to be able to login.

    • Added notification when inhibition effect begins and ends
    • It is no longer possible to enter the enemy’s
      • Farm
      • Barracks if your level is higher than 60
    • The following dialogs can now be transparent
      • System menu
      • Advanced menu
      • Skills dialog
      • Bag
    • Show buffs now works with slates
    • Added option to set a client instance title to include the character name which the client is currently logged into. This is particularly useful when using multiple clients. Example: “Helbreath Crusade – GMShine”
    • Added /party [playername] command to party with a player without having to click on them. Note that they should still be on the same map and be in peace mode. Example: “/party GMShine” will ask GMShine to let you into the party
    • Added /unparty command to leave a party without having to browse your way through the menus
    • Lucky Gold Ring special ability activated, 10% chance of escaping death or drop.
    • Bishop Boots will, from now on, be activated slightly differently. They will conserve their endurance bonus from manufacturing after the conversion, so boots created with a high manufacturing skill (above 32%) will have higher endurance.
    • Increased the endurance on Boots and Shoes
    • Reduced MA / MR on
      • Ettin
      • Werewolves
      • Demons
      • Unicorns
      • Gargoyles
    • Middlelands map pit locations redone
    • Adjusted the Dye bottles to show their color more correctly
    • Adjusted the drop list to allow Barbarian Battle Hammers and Black Shadow Swords to drop.
    • Enabled amounts greater than 64k to be shown on the client.
    • New weapon drops have been added as follows:
      • Barbarian Hammer drops instead of Battle Hammer from Ettins.
      • Battle Hammer drops instead of Hammer from Ettins and Mountain Giants.
      • Hammer drops instead of Mysterious Tokens from Ogre, Werewold, Stalker, Dark Elf and Ice Golem.
      • Black Shadow Sword drops instead of Flamberge +1, from Demon, Unicorn, Gargoyle, Helclaw and Tigerworm.
        • Mysterious Tokens and Flamberge +1 will no longer drop from monsters.
