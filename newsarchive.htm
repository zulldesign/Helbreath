  • Jun 28, 2013

    +Player Portal Update - Ability to unlink accounts


    We’ve implemented the ability to unlink accounts. If you’re the account owner, and have since changed the password, you can go to your profile (link below) and unlink the account! This means that if you’ve given your account information to someone else to sync up on the portal, you can now have a little more control and revoke their access.

    https://www.apphb.com/portal/profile.htm

    Many thanks to Trinny!

    Remember: iEnt does not support account sharing.

  • Jun 21, 2013

    +Happy 4th July! Event and update to EXP Capes


    Happy 4th July!

    As a treat we’ve increased the EXP of red, white and blue monsters. We’re also offering a FREE set of 20% EXP shoes, obtainable only through the IN GAME store (Use the Auto Updater to make sure you have the updated config files!). You can buy as many pairs as you want! Just remember they’ll be deleted in approx 2 Monday resets.

    Due to limitations – We recommend that you buy them on a non-main character so that when we place the stats on the items throughout the weekend, the changes get saved (the character needs to be offline, this is easiest to do if you put the items on an alternate character). Do not trade items for them! They will be deleted.

    Obtainable through the shop in game and activated once the script has been manually run.

    We have also changed the way the EXP capes are appearing in the online store. You now buy 1 per character (instead of a once off price that covers all characters on the account at the time). The capes are  non-transferable and bound to the character they are bought on – the one you login on.
