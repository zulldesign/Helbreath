Rules



While you are an online member of the HelbreathUSA Online Service, you must abide by the following rules. IF YOU BREAK ANY OF THESE RULES, YOUR HelbreathUSA ONLINE ACCOUNT, AND YOUR RIGHT TO USE THE HelbreathUSA ONLINE SERVICE, MAY BE TERMINATED IMMEDIATELY BY HelbreathUSA. IF THIS HAPPENS, YOU WILL NOT BE ENTITLED TO ANY REFUND OF ANY AMOUNTS PAID BY YOU TO USE THE HelbreathUSA ONLINE SERVICE AND YOU WILL FORFEIT ALL OF YOUR UNUSED ACCESS TIME.

  1. The usage of profanity or vulgarity is not allowed. This includes public, town, guild, local chat and whispers. You may not use any offensive or sexually explicit language, or use variations of excessive capital letters and ASCII characters to avoid the in game chat filter. You may not use any unlawful, harmful, threatening, abusive, harassing, defamatory, vulgar, obscene, hateful, racially, ethnically or otherwise objectionable language toward any other player.
  2. Vulgar names, or names with a profanity will be changed to numbers.
  3. The harassment of players or staff is forbidden. Including but not limited to irritating, tormenting persistently, going above and beyond to impede the a person(s) from the normal playability of the game. To wear out and or exhaust a person. Repeatedly target a person beyond what is deemed normal playability and happenstance. Targeting a specific player rather than character. You may not harass, threaten, embarrass or cause distress, unwanted attention or discomfort to another player. Such harassment is determined by Administration at IENT. If you feel you are being harassed please take screen shots and submit them with a TIXX at http://www.helsupport.com.
  4. Away from keyboard macroing to gain skills or experience is not allowed. This includes taping down the mouse or any other actions that allow your character to complete an action while you are not at the computer.
  5. Game hacks are not allowed, this includes but is not limited to altering the client or image (PAK) files in order to boost your character in any fashion. You will not attempt to interfere with, hack into, or decipher any transmissions to or from the servers running the HelbreathUSA Online Service.
  6. You may not impersonate any HelbreathUSA Online Customer Support representative or other employee of HelbreathUSA. This includes making characters with the prefix or suffix of "GM". All official Game Masters have a shield that glows with the letters "GM" on them.
  7. You may not market, promote or advertise, or make any other form of solicitation (including pyramid schemes and chain letters) on the HelbreathUSA Online Service or through the HelbreathUSA Online Web Site.
  8. You may not violate any local, state, national or international law or regulation.
  9. You may not modify any part of the HelbreathUSA Online Service or Web Site that HelbreathUSA does not specifically authorize you to modify. This includes but is not limited to editing *.pak [image] files, sound files and game appearance.
  10. You may not arrange for the exchange or transfer of any pirated software or other contraband while you are on the HelbreathUSA Online Service or Web Site, or use the HelbreathUSA Online Service or Web Site for any other illegal purpose.
  11. You may not organize any guilds or groups that are based on, or espouse, any racist, sexist, anti-religious, anti-ethnic, anti-gay, or other hate-mongering philosophy.
  12. You may not use the HelbreathUSA Online Service or the HelbreathUSA Online Web Site for any activities other than activities that are permitted within the game world.
  13. You may not post or communicate any employee's or player's real world personal information within the HelbreathUSA Online Service or through the HelbreathUSA Online Web Site or bulletin board Helbreath Portal (http://www.Hbportal.net).
  14. You may not give false information or intentionally hide any information, such as email address or billing information, which is required at the time you register with the HelbreathUSA Online Service. It is your responsibility to keep this information up-to-date using the web site located at http://secure.ient.com/am/. Do not share this information with others. Doing so is at your own risk. Real GM's will never ask for your password.
  15. You will not upload or transmit on the HelbreathUSA Online Service, or through the HelbreathUSA Online Web Site or bulletin board, any copyrighted content that you do not own all rights to, unless you have the express written permission of the author or copyright holder.
  16. You will not exploit any bug in the HelbreathUSA Online product or the HelbreathUSA Online Service to gain unfair advantage in the game and you will not communicate the existence of any such bug (either directly or through public posting) to any other user of the HelbreathUSA Online Service. Promptly report any such bug via gamemaster@helbreathusa.com email address. This includes but is not limited to the "Civilian Bug" where the enemy town is unable to detect members of the same town while a Civilian is on the screen and taking advantage of the civilian status to interrupt, bump or other wise hinder an enemy raid.
  17. You will not attempt to play HelbreathUSA Online on any service that is not controlled or authorized by HelbreathUSA.
  18. You will not create, use or provide any server emulator or other site where HelbreathUSA Online may be played, and you will not post or distribute any utilities, emulators or other software tools related to HelbreathUSA Online without the express written permission of HelbreathUSA.
  19. You will not do anything else that interferes with the ability of other HelbreathUSA Online users to enjoy playing the game in accordance with its rules, or that increases the expense or difficulty of HelbreathUSA in maintaining the HelbreathUSA Online Service for the enjoyment of all its users.
  20. You may not disrupt the normal playability of a zone or area. Zone/Area Disruption is defined as any activity designed to harm or inconvenience an individual or group(s) of players in a way that the game does not allow. This includes, but is not limited to:
    • Continually killing players of the same fraction on a safe map.*
    • Blocking any doorway of any building. Blocking within a map with the intention of impairing the normal playability of the zone.
      • If there is another way to enter the map and a player(s) is disrupting one entrance, this is not considered blocking.
      • During a Crusade or Siege it's "all out war". Blocking doors, entrances to maps and pathways is a legal tactic.
    • Boxing a player is legal everywhere, except if it results in blocking of a doorway to a building.
    • Bringing summons from the spell "Summon-Creature", Ogre Pots or any summon scrolls to the entrances of the Promise Lands and the Beginner Dungeon.
      • Area effect spells such as "Cloud-Kill" and "Poison-Cloud" are legal, as is the use of "Fire Bows".
    • Refusing to cooperate with the other parties at a contested spawn site or a Game Master's (GM's) event after having been instructed to do so by a HelbreathUSA representative.
    • Making excessive [spam] and inappropriate use of public channels of communications in general, town, guild, local and whispers.
    • Intentionally causing excessive zone latency (creating excessive corpses, abusing spell effects, etc.).
    • Causing intentional experience loss to other players (deliberately impeding fleeing players by blocking their escape route, intentionally training NPC's on other players, etc.).
      If you feel some one is disrupting the normal playability of a zone or area, please take screen shots and submit them with a TIXX at http://www.helsupport.com.
      * Any map where you are sent to jail for killing a member of town where the repetitive killing of any person by a singular person or guild is considered a "safe map" and could result in further action. Maps protected by the jail penalty are designed for leveling, and players should be allowed to do so without the domination of another player(s) killing them by PK (Same town). This is determined by Administration. Any map where you remain on the map for killing a member of town is deemed to be part of game mechanics and to be handled within the game by players. Maps that are not protected by the jail penalty are designed for hunting, and players should be able to do so with the knowledge that they are in a dangerous map. This is determined by Administration. As long as no 1 person or guild is being hunted down and killed specifically because they are that person or targeted because they are in that guild. Pit etiquette* is to be expected but not required by the person entering the area. As long as 1 group of people do not dominate an area full time. This is determined by Administration. * Asking if they mind your presence, reacting on their reply as you see fit.
    • You may not abuse the ability to have multiple clients in order to spy on the other town.
  21. You may not defraud other players. Fraud is defined as falsely representing one's intentions to make a gain at another's expense, this is determined by Administration. Examples of this activity include, but are not limited to, using deception to deprive another player of items, slandering another player or impersonating them with the intention of causing harm to that player's reputation, or falsely representing one's identity in order to gain access to another player's items, account or account information also referred to as scamming.
  22. Sharing items in game is not supported by IENT, doing so is at your own risk. If you share your items with some one who shares their account unbeknownst to you, it is still your responsibility having given the items in the first place. We reserve the right to act on each report as necessary and treat each one as required. IENT takes no responsibility for lost or stolen items if you share your account. Accidentally selling items or passing them to another player is the responsibility of the player.
    • IEntertainment offer a "confirmation screenshot" service for people who want to share items. You, the lender are required to take a screenshot of the person you are lending your item to and have them clearly state that they will return the item when you request it. Screenshots MUST be kept up-to-date (within a 2 week time period) other wise they risk being invalid. You need to have proof that the item is being lent and not given. This does not protect you if the item is unintentionally lost by the sharer. If the item is lost accidently through a mis-click, the item can not be restored.
  23. We reserve the right to change, disband or delete offensive Character and Guild names or profiles without prior notification of the account holder.
  24. Laming is the organizing of the enemy to benefit yourself or your town. This is split in to two different sections.
    • EKs are Enemy Kills and are used for getting Hero Gear and event items. Laming EKs is when the another character from the opposite fraction assists in being killed to facilitate the gaining of the EKs which are used to collect Hero Gear. Whether an EK is lamed is determined by GM1 through the use of game logs. Laming EKs is punished by the removal of all EKs and any Hero Gear previously gained on the account, not just the character. Repeat offenders will have a 20% exp removal. Kill toys, the characters which are killed in order to get the EKs will be locked and possibly deleted regardless of items held on the character.
    • Crusade Points - During the crusade points are given by killing the enemy, random enemy town summons, enemy buildings and enemy units. Laming crusade points by organizing a member of the enemy town to create buildings with the intention of being killed by yourself or your guild or your fellow town people is laming points. Guilds who take advantage of the game mechanics by abusing in such a manner will have the Guild Master sent to bleeding island to break the connection between the summons and the Guild Master. If the abuse continues the Guild Master account will be locked for a minimum of a week and the guild will be disband.
  25. "It was a joke.." or "I didn't know" - is not viable as an excuse. Saying or doing anything that is normally punishable under our rules is still punishable despite the fact that you and the other person involved may have thought it all humorous or didn't know. No one else is in on the joke so it is taken seriously and dealt with in accordance with the other rules listed here.
  26. Item restoration is possible if iEntertainment is informed within 24 hours of the item being either sold to the Blacksmith or Shop Keeper. Scroll restoration is also possible if iEntertainment is informed within 24 hours of the item being depleted / learnt by the character - In some cases a restoration scroll may be required from the online store. IEntertainment can not restore any items lost due to misplacement EG: Dropping on the ground or passed to another person (accidentally or intentional). Items lost due to sharing the item and or account information is not covered by this rule.
  27. IEntertainment offers a slate restoration service to any slates lost due to disconnection. IEntertainment must be notified within 24 hours of the lost slate via TIXX at http://www.helsupport.com.
  28. You may not abuse your characters civilian stats in order to benefit or harm in a PvP situation. While iEnt understands that spying happens, actively taking part in any PvP situation in order to interrupt it intentionally will result in punishment. Using your civilian to watch the PvP is included, use a traveler instead.
  29. Harassment to a player or intentionally causing unwanted stress to a player beyond what is deemed acceptable to administration, while they have a slate activated is not allowed.
    This includes but is not limited to:
    • Experience Slates- Luring monsters out of a pit to cause an experience loss and or disrupting the normal playability of the zone with the intention of disrupting or reducing the experience gained while on the slate. Being EKed while on an experience slate is a risk that a player takes when using a slate.
    • Mana Slates- Intentionally causing grief to the person who has the slate activated. Being EKed while on a mana slate is a risk that a player takes when using a slate.
    • Health Slate - Due to the nature of the Health slate giving the player invincibility from damage except when AOE spells are used and no endurance loss from armor, it is deemed "fair play" that the Slater becomes susceptible to some forms of distraction. However, if the person using the slate becomes harassed due to using the slate, warnings can be issued under the harassment rule and deal with accordingly.
      • "Area of Effect" spells that do damage to the slate user despite the infinite hit points are not to be excessively used on the Slater and are to be limited to a hinderance, determined by administration.
    • Berserk Slate - Attempting to cause the character to disconnect or otherwise cancel the effect of the slate. Being EKed while on a berserk slate is a risk that a player takes when using a slate.
    • IEntertainment and Helbreath Staff will restore slates that are lost due to a random disconnection however, will not restore them for intentional disconnections or termination of connection to avoid being EKed or to extend the use.
  30. The selling or trading of in game items for real currency or other game items is strictly prohibited, punishable by ban.

If an infraction of any rule occurs the account is still liable for any punishment due. "It was my brother.." or friend or the family parrot does not matter. IENT doesn't support the sharing of accounts. By giving some one your account information, you are allowing them to do with your account as they wish. We will not restore, refund or place the punishments of their actions on their accounts. The account it happens on is the account that is punished. Each warning sent to you is also noted down on your account and can be valid for at least 3 months after the warning was issued. Multiple warnings within a short time span will result in more severe punishment. The goal of the rules is to enforce a fun and enjoyable play time for all.
