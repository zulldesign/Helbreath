My ASP Affiliate Program is the easiest, fastest and cheapest way to run an affiliate program on your website.

Completely idiot proof, you should have no trouble installing and using it within a matter of minutes. But please read the requirements carefuly.

M.A.A.P is written in ASP/VBscript using Macromedia Ultradev, it is 100% customizable and extendible. Easy straight forward setup with only two lines of code to write.

Based on a fixed commission fee structure ($xx/sale), it's best suited for small websites with a few products. PayPal users only can now give % commission !!

M.A.A.P will not track individual product sales... only the sale itself. Customers are tracked using cookies, once they buy something, irrespective of price, quantity or product and go to the "thank you page" affiliates get their commissions.
